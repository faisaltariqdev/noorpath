Teaching Method

How We Teach Duas to Kids

Children learn duas best through repetition, stories, and understanding the meaning. Our tutors make each dua come alive with:

  • Listen & Repeat — Tutor recites first, child repeats until fluent
  • Meaning explained — Child understands what they're saying to Allah
  • Real-life context — When to say each dua, stories behind it
  • Practice cards — PDF practice sheets sent to parents weekly
  • Star rewards — Gamified system to motivate children

The Prophet ﷺ on Teaching Children

"Every one of you is a shepherd and is responsible for his flock... A man is the shepherd of his household and is responsible for his flock."
