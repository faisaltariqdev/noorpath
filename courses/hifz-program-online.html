Our Hifz System

The NoorPath Hifz System

A proven 3-part daily system used by Huffaz around the world — adapted perfectly for online learning.

📝

Sabaq (New Lesson)

Each session begins with memorizing new verses. The tutor listens, corrects pronunciation, and helps you memorize securely before moving forward.

🔄

Sabaqi (Recent Revision)

Revise the last 7–10 days of memorization to strengthen recently learned portions before they fade from memory.

📚

Manzil (Full Revision)

Weekly full revision of all previously memorized portions to ensure long-term retention of every Juz.
