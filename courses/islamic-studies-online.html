Subjects Covered

What We Teach in Islamic Studies

🌙

Aqeedah (Islamic Belief)

6 Pillars of Faith, Tawheed (Oneness of Allah), names and attributes of Allah, belief in angels, books, prophets, Day of Judgment, and Qadar.

📜

Fiqh (Islamic Law)

Purification (Taharah), prayer (Salah), fasting (Sawm), Zakat, Hajj basics, halal/haram, everyday rulings for Muslim life.

🕌

Seerah (Prophet's Life ﷺ)

Complete life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ — birth, Prophethood, Hijra, battles, character, family, and his lasting legacy for mankind.

📿

Hadith Studies

Selection of authentic Hadith from Arbaeen Nawawi and Riyad al-Salihin. Understanding context, application, and benefits.

🤝

Islamic Manners (Adab)

Etiquettes of eating, sleeping, talking, greeting, dealing with parents, neighbours, and non-Muslims based on Sunnah.

🌍

Islamic History

Khilafah Rashidah, golden age of Islam, great Islamic scholars, rise of Islamic civilization and its contribution to the world.
