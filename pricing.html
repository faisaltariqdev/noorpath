Common Questions

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. Families can book a free trial class before enrolling in a monthly plan.

Can siblings share a plan?

Yes. Family plans are designed for siblings and offer better value than separate enrollments.

Which courses are included?

Plans can be used for Quran reading, Noorani Qaida, Tajweed, Hifz, Arabic, Islamic studies, and selected kids programs depending on your learning path.

Do adults and children have different pricing?

Pricing depends more on class frequency and family structure than age. Adults and children can both begin with a free trial.
