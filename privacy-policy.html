Privacy Policy

How NoorPath Academy collects, uses, stores, and protects information submitted by parents, students, and website visitors.

Effective date: 21 June 2026

NoorPath Academy respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect through our website, trial booking forms, WhatsApp, and email, and how we use that information to provide online Quran classes and support to families worldwide.

1. Information We Collect

  • Parent or student name
  • Email address and WhatsApp/phone number
  • Country, state, or time-zone information
  • Course interest such as Tajweed, Hifz, Qaida, kids Quran, or Islamic studies
  • Details you voluntarily provide in contact forms, WhatsApp messages, or trial bookings

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to trial requests and support questions
  • To match students with suitable tutors and schedules
  • To communicate class details, reminders, and follow-up messages
  • To improve our courses, landing pages, and website experience
  • To maintain internal records for student support and service quality

3. Contact Forms and Third-Party Processing

Our website may use trusted third-party form tools and communication platforms to receive trial requests and messages. Information submitted through these forms is used only for handling your inquiry, booking, and student communication. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

4. Children’s Privacy

NoorPath Academy serves children through parents or guardians. We expect parents or guardians to submit booking information on behalf of minors. If you believe information about a child has been submitted improperly, contact us and we will review the request promptly.

5. Cookies and Website Analytics

We may use basic cookies, platform logs, or performance tools to understand traffic, improve page speed, and optimize user experience. This data is used for website improvement and not for resale.

6. Data Retention

We keep inquiry and trial information only as long as reasonably necessary for communication, follow-up, record keeping, or service delivery. If you want your information removed from our contact records, you may request deletion by email.

7. Security

We use HTTPS and reasonable website security practices to protect submitted information. However, no internet transmission method is completely risk-free, so users should share only information necessary for class booking and communication.

8. Your Rights

You may contact us to request correction or deletion of information you have submitted, subject to reasonable record-keeping needs.

9. Contact

If you have any privacy-related questions, contact NoorPath Academy at info@noorpath.online or on WhatsApp at +92 312 4877906.
