Every challenge below is something we built NoorPath to fix.

The problem

One child is on Qaida, another on Tajweed, another starting Hifz — they cannot share the same class.

Our solution: Each sibling gets their own tutor and personalized roadmap.

The problem

Scheduling chaos

School, homework, time zones — juggling multiple class times is exhausting for parents.

Our solution: Back-to-back slots and one timezone — we plan around your family routine.

The problem

No clear progress

You are not sure what each child learned this week or whether they are improving.

Our solution: Weekly parent reports for every child — all in one place.

The problem

Our solution: Family plans cover up to 3–5 members at one affordable monthly rate.

Our solution: Certified female tutors available for every child on request.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior knowledge of Arabic or the Quran?
Absolutely not! We welcome complete beginners. Our tutors are experienced in teaching people of all ages and backgrounds, starting from the very basics of Arabic letters and pronunciation.
How does the free trial class work?
After registration, you will be matched with a suitable tutor and scheduled for a 30-minute trial session. This session is completely free with no credit card required. If you are satisfied, you can then choose a subscription plan.
Are female tutors available?
Yes! We have a large pool of certified female Quran teachers. You can request a female tutor during registration or at any point during your subscription.
What platform is used for online classes?
Classes are conducted via Zoom, Skype, or Google Meet — whichever you prefer. We also offer our own NoorPath Learning Platform which features shared whiteboard, Quran display, and recording features built in.
Can I change my tutor if I am not satisfied?
Of course. Your comfort and learning experience are our priority. You can request a tutor change at any time through your student dashboard — no questions asked.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes. We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee on all subscription plans. If you are not completely satisfied with your learning experience, we will refund 100% of your payment.
What age groups do you teach?
We teach students from age 4 to adult. We have specialized tutors for young children who use engaging, age-appropriate methods, as well as experienced teachers for adult learners including seniors and reverts.
Can I enroll multiple siblings at different levels?
Yes. Each sibling is assessed separately and placed on the right roadmap — Qaida, Nazra, Tajweed, Hifz, or Daily Duas. Each child gets their own tutor and schedule. Family and Scholar plans cover 3–5 siblings at one affordable rate.
Does each child get their own tutor?
Yes. Every student has dedicated 1-on-1 classes with a certified tutor matched to their age and level. Female tutors are available for daughters and young children on request.
Do you offer family or sibling discounts?
Yes. Our Family plan (up to 3 siblings) and Scholar plan (up to 5 siblings) cost far less than separate Starter enrollments per child. Mention siblings when you book your free trial.
Do you teach daily duas to children?
Yes. All children in our Kids and Little Noor programs learn essential daily duas and adhkar — including morning/evening remembrances, before eating, sleeping, traveling, and more. Each dua is taught in Arabic with transliteration and a simple English meaning so children understand what they are saying.
What is the All-in-One Kids course?
Our All-in-One program combines Noorani Qaida, daily duas, and child-friendly Hadith — plus lessons on loving and serving parents (bir al-walidayn). Children gain Quran skills, Islamic knowledge, and strong character in one structured course.
