Founder Vision

Why a Software Engineer Built an Online Quran Academy

Many families want Quran education that is authentic, consistent, flexible, and easy to trust. NoorPath Academy was built to solve that problem with strong teaching standards and modern learning systems.

Faisal Tariq's software engineering background shapes how NoorPath works: clear onboarding, structured course roadmaps, secure communication, parent-friendly updates, and a smoother online trial experience. The goal is not just to offer classes, but to create a reliable learning system families can continue with confidence.

That is why NoorPath focuses on one-to-one Quran classes, qualified male and female tutors, weekly progress visibility, family plans for siblings, and practical courses from Noorani Qaida to Tajweed, Hifz, Arabic, Islamic Studies, daily duas, and Salah basics.

A Message to Parents

Quran Learning Should Feel Clear, Safe, and Consistent

A good online academy should not leave parents confused. It should give families clarity before enrollment and confidence after every class.

  • Clear course choices for kids, adults, reverts, and families with multiple siblings.
  • Flexible class schedules for families in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, Pakistan, and worldwide.
  • Male and female tutor options so families can choose the most comfortable setup.
  • Direct WhatsApp and email communication so parents are never left guessing.
